                                   SEPT       *AUG
 Balance ($ billion)                8.9        4.3
 Exports ($ billion)               43.5       39.2
         (pct growth vs yr ago)    -8.3      -14.9

    NOTES:
    - Reuters poll: Exports were seen down 10.0 percent in
September over a year earlier while imports were seen down 18.1
percent. [ID:nL4N11V07Y]
    - The average export value per working day stood at $2.02
billion in September, compared with a revised $1.78 billion in
August, Thomson Reuters calculations show. 
    - China is South Korea&apos;s largest export market, taking about
one-quarter of shipments, followed by the United States and the
European Union.   
    - By product, electronics goods account for over one-fifth
of the country&apos;s total exports, followed chemical products and
industrial machinery.
